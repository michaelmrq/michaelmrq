Noise-Cancelling PC Fan

The noise-cancelling PC fan uses a piezoelectric device to generate real-time anti-sound signals, reducing tonal noise. A controller optimizes suppression while preserving cooling performance.

Drag Reduction on Trucks

The drag reduction is achieved using plasma actuators to control airflow at different speeds and turns, minimizing separation drag. This advanced technology reduces fuel consumption and emissions without impacting vehicle performance.

AI Airport Traffic Management

The AI Airport Traffic Management system uses advanced algorithms to optimize ground operations in real-time, reducing delays and CO2 emissions. It streamlines the movement of aircraft and vehicles through the taxiways, ensuring efficient and sustainable airport workflows.
